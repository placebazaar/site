About PlaceBazaar

Placebazaar is a platform where contributors are paid to maintain and add data to a decentralized, worldwide map of roads, trails, cafés, public transport and much more.

PlaceBazaar connects parties in need of mapping data, with contributors of that data, on a marketplace. Built on top of the fantastic OpenStreetmap data, we ensure the data is uncensurable and the contributors are paid.

The database is distributed on IPFs, a decentral, uncensurable filesystem, allowing unlimited access to this global, Open Source, mapping data. Contributors can add any data and get paid in PBZ tokens by those in need of data. A paid review process allows consumers to ensure validity of the data.

The Marketplace

Requests and offers of worldwide mapping data
01

Request

Need new or better mapping data? Or want a place verified? Place an offer to pay PBZ tokens for such data on the marketplace.

02

Contribute

Mappers see these requests and provide the data, through an app or an online editor. Optionally, proof of the data can be uploaded.

03

Review

Reviewers acknowledge or reject the data. Once acknowledged, the contributor and reviewer is paid by the Smart Contract.
